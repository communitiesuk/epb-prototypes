How is this calculated?
The calculation is made by working out how much energy a typical 2-person household would use in this property over a year. This is then multiplied by average energy prices in the region over a 6-month period. The prices were last updated in October 2025.

What does this involve?
This involves adding insulation under the roof structure or above the ceiling.

What does this involve?
This involves installing solar panels on the roof, and an inverter which connects to the property’s electrical system.
