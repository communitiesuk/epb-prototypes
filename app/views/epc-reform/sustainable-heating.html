What does the rating mean?
Properties get a score from 100 (best) to 1 (worst). A better score means the property is more energy efficient and produces fewer carbon emissions.   The score is based on the energy efficiency and carbon emissions of a property’s heating, hot water and cooking.
  • A rating - 90 to 100
  • B rating - 80 to 89
  • C rating - 70 to 79
  • D rating - 60 to 69
  • E rating - 50 to 59
  • F rating - 40 to 49
  • G rating - 1 to 39
