Find an energy assessor for a residential property

You need a valid Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) before you sell or let a residential property.

Use this service to find a qualified assessor to measure the energy efficiency of your property, or the property of someone you represent. The assessor will create an EPC.

If your property is in Scotland, you should use the Scottish government’s EPC service.

You do not need an EPC if the:

  • property is not residential
  • property is temporarily residential, with a planned residential use of 2 years or less
  • property’s new owner intends to demolish it and never use it as a residential property
