Find an energy certificate or report for a non-domestic property

Use this service to find energy certificates and reports for non-domestic properties in England, Wales and Northern Ireland.

Non-domestic properties are commercial and public properties.

For non-domestic properties, you can view their:

  • Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) and Recommendation Report (EPC-RR)
  • Display Energy Certificate (DEC) and Recommendation Report (DEC-RR)
  • Air Conditioning Inspection Report (AC-REPORT) and Certificate (AC-CERT)
This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

Before you start

If you need additional support to find an energy certificate or report, call our helpdesk on 0233 788 1234 (Monday to Friday, 9am - 5pm).

For non-domestic properties in Scotland, use the Scottish government's EPC and DEC service.
