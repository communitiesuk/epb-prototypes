Primary energy use

The primary energy use for this property per year is 74 kilowatt hours per square metre (kWh/m2).

What is primary energy use?

Primary energy use is a measure of the energy required for lighting, heating and hot water in a property. The calculation includes:

  • the efficiency of the property’s heating system
  • power station efficiency for electricity
  • the energy used to produce the fuel and deliver it to the property

Installing a smart meter will help you to understand more about how you use energy in your home and may help you to reduce your energy bills. For information about smart meters visit Smart Energy GB.
